Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before booking with us.

Can we customize the itinerary completely? add
Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke travel. If you want a private candlelit dinner in Alleppey, we arrange it precisely to your tastes.
Do you provide airport transfers from CCJ? add
Yes — we offer premium airport transfers from Calicut International Airport (CCJ) ensuring a stress-free start and end to every journey.
What is the best time to book a honeymoon from Kozhikode? add
We recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks in advance for peak season destinations. Our team will guide you based on your chosen destination and budget.
Do tours start directly from Thamarassery? add
Yes — our group trekking tours begin from Thamarassery, the gateway to Wayanad, with expert-led expeditions into untouched wilderness.
What fitness level is required for group treks? add
We offer treks for all fitness levels. Each expedition listing specifies the difficulty. Our guides adapt pace to ensure everyone has a rewarding experience.
What is the best time to visit Wayanad for trekking? add
October to February offers ideal trekking conditions in Wayanad — cooler temperatures, clear skies, and lush post-monsoon landscapes.
